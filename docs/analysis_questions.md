# Data-Mining Analysis Questions

## Round 1: Coverage and Integrity

- Which cities have speed, rainfall, TMC, demand, and network files?
- Which files are too large for naive loading?
- Which city-months or city-years are represented?

## Round 2: Disruption and Recovery Signal

- Do rainfall peaks correspond to measurable speed deficits?
- Are speed deficits spatially heterogeneous across links/TMCs?
- Are there recovery trajectories after selected rainfall events?

## Round 3: Functional Dependence

- Do OD demand and link performance data contain enough structure to approximate origin-destination dependence?
- Are functional losses concentrated in high-volume or high-centrality network elements?
- Are cities structurally different enough to support cross-city law extraction?

## Round 4: Fit to Paper Idea

- Which parts of recoverable resilience are directly data-supported?
- Which parts are inferential but plausible?
- Which parts must wait for the optimization model?
